The Level and its Journey

What's the niche-est level you know? For me, it's the iCryzstal Challenge. I mean- I don't even know where to start. The famous YouTuber **npesta**. Whom is renowned in the community as one of the greatest players in the games history. But did you know he was a creator too? He made the level Kenos, Gizbro challenge, and so much more. So I decided to make a mod that allows us to truly appreciate the level's nicheness. It's brought over 55 thousand people together in challenge and slightly below average transitions. I wish that someone  could appreciate this level as much as I do.
